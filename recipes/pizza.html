Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian, specifically Neapolitan, dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • yeast
  • flour
  • water
  • salt
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

Step-by-step

  1. combine the warm water, sugar, and instant yeast. Mix just to combine and set aside for 5-10 minutes, until frothy.
  2. Add the olive oil, milk, salt, and 3 cups flour to the stand mixer bowl.
  3. Cover to rest 20 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into 2-3 pieces. Place in a greased baking pan, drizzle the tops with a little olive oil, then cover with plastic wrap
  5. Preheat the oven to 500F for 30 minutes. Place the pizza rack in the oven to heat, as well.
  6. Carefully remove the pizza rack from the oven and place on a heat-safe surface.
  7. Add about 3/4 cups shredded mozzarella, 3-4 fresh mozzarella slices, and freshly grated pecorino or parmesan.
  8. Bake for 8-12 minutes
  9. Garnish with more freshly grated pecorino or parmesan, chopped basil, and cracked black pepper.
